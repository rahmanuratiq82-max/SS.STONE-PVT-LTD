DATA PROTECTION

Privacy Policy for SS Stone

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

SS Stone ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and clients. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out forms (like the 'Get a Quote' form), send us emails, or contact us via telephone.

  • **Personal Identification Data:** Name, company name, email address, phone number.
  • **Project Data:** Details about your project requirements (stone type, quantity, intended use).
  • **Log Data:** Information automatically collected when you visit our site, such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for business purposes related to your inquiry or our services:

  • To provide and manage the services you request (e.g., sending a quote).
  • To communicate with you regarding your enquiry.
  • To improve our website's performance and service offerings.
  • For internal record-keeping and regulatory compliance.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information may be shared with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Data Security

We employ a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you submit a request or access your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Consent

By using our site and submitting information, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

Contact Information
